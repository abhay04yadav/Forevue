A single data figure with an uppercase label — Public Sans 800 with tabular numerals. Numbers never use the serif.

```jsx
<DataStat label="Students watched" value="1,240" />
<DataStat label="Below attendance line" value="6" delta="2" deltaDir="down" sub="this week" />
```

`deltaDir` colours the delta: `up` green, `down` red, `flat` muted. Note for risk contexts a "down" trend may be good — pick the direction by meaning, not sign.
