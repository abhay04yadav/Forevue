# Forevue — Brand & Design Reference

**Tagline:** "Clarity, early."
**What Forevue is:** the intelligence layer for Indian higher education. It sits on top of the systems a college already runs (the ERP), lets staff ask anything in plain English, watches every student for early signs of risk, and keeps accreditation evidence ready. It does not replace the ERP; it lets you see inside it.

> **How to use this file when generating a design system:** This describes our brand. There are TWO layers — please respect the split:
> - **PRODUCT UI (in-app)** → use **Public Sans** for everything, plus the teal + neutral tokens below. This is what the design system should mainly produce.
> - **MARKETING ONLY (deck, website, social)** → may add **Spectral** (serif) for large headlines and the warm "paper" surface. **Do NOT use the serif inside the product UI / dashboards.**
> The shared **Teal #0E7C86** is the through-line that unifies both.

---

## 1. Personality
Four traits: **Clear** (plain over clever), **Grounded** (shows its work, never hypes), **Incisive** (calm but gets to the point), **Respectful** (human-in-command, privacy-first).
Because Forevue handles minors' data and flags struggling students, the feel must be **trustworthy and humane** — never "disrupt your campus with AI" energy.

## 2. Colour

| Name | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Forevue Teal | `#0E7C86` | **Primary / actions.** Shared with the product — the brand spine. |
| Midnight Teal | `#06363B` | Dark hero canvases, depth (marketing). |
| Deep Teal | `#0A656D` | Links, secondary, focus rings. |
| Insight Amber | `#E6A23C` | The single accent — the "moment of clarity." Use **sparingly**, one per view, **never as a background**. |
| Ink | `#0E1B22` | Text on light. |
| Paper | `#F7F5F0` | Warm light surface (marketing). In-app surfaces stay cooler/neutral. |
| Mist | `#5E7175` | Muted / secondary text. |

**Rules:** Teal carries everything and matches the product so brand and app read as one. Amber is the spark only. **Risk colours (red / amber / green tiers) are PRODUCT-ONLY** — used inside the app for student risk states (colour-vision-safe, always paired with a shape + label). They never enter the marketing brand. Ink-on-Paper and white-on-Midnight-Teal both pass AA contrast.

## 3. Typography
- **Public Sans** — all UI, body, labels, and data. The product face. Numbers use **tabular numerals**. (Weights 400–800.)
- **Spectral** (serif) — display/headlines and brand lines in **marketing only**. Calm, literary, scholarly. (Weights 400–700, incl. italic for the tagline.)

Type roles: Display = Spectral 600 · Tagline = Spectral italic · Body = Public Sans 400–500 · UI eyebrow labels = Public Sans 700 uppercase · Data = Public Sans 800 tabular.

## 4. Logo / mark — "the resolve"
Scattered records converge to a single bright point that looks forward — *fore + vue*, foresight. Teal lines, one amber focal point. Real vector SVG assets exist (icon, wordmark, lockup; light/dark/mono; app icon; favicon). Don't stretch, recolour outside the palette, add shadows, or crowd it. Clear space = the icon's own height. Minimum lockup width ~120px; below that use the icon alone.

## 5. Voice & messaging
- Plain verbs, sentence case.
- Lead with the specific ("Six students in CSE-3," not "actionable insights").
- Put the evidence on the table — every claim names what it's based on.
- Dignity for every student — never alarmist, never a label without care.
- Confident, never boastful. No emoji, no "revolutionary," no hype.

**In voice:** "Six students in CSE-3 slipped below the attendance line this week. Here's why, and who to talk to."
**Off voice:** "🚀 Our AI-powered platform empowers institutions to leverage data-driven insights!"

**Say this, not that:**
- "Forevue surfaces / shows / flags" — not "decides / acts / fixes"
- "students who need attention" — not "failures / problem students"
- "the systems you already run" — not "rip and replace your ERP"
- "with the evidence behind it" — not unexplained scores
- "consent-gated · minor — consent required" — never share a minor's data quietly

## 6. Signature device & imagery
Use **the resolve** (scattered points → one forward line) as the recurring motif: deck opener, website hero, app splash, loading states. One memorable element; keep everything around it quiet.
**Imagery:** real Indian campus texture and evidence/citation motifs — never glowing-blue-AI-brain stock. **Icons:** simple line icons, rounded caps, single weight, teal or ink. Avoid gradient-mesh purple, node-network logos, and "empower with AI" clichés.

## 7. UI component direction (for the product design system)
- Calm, utilitarian, information-dense but uncluttered. Public Sans throughout.
- Cards: white surfaces, soft neutral borders, generous radius, subtle shadow.
- One primary (teal) action per view; secondary actions are bordered/quiet.
- Student risk shown as colour **+ shape + label** (never colour alone): high = square, watch = diamond, low = circle.
- Every AI answer shows its **evidence/provenance** (grounded-or-abstain — if it can't ground an answer, it says so rather than guessing).
- Generated content is always a **human-owned draft** (advisory; never auto-finalized; no write-back to the ERP).
- Minors always carry a **consent-required** marker (DPDP).

## 8. Posture (must hold across everything)
Advisory (it surfaces, humans decide) · Grounded (always shows evidence) · Privacy-first (minors consent-gated). **Never imply Forevue acts on its own.**
